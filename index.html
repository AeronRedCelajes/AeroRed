About Me

Hello World! I'm Aeron Red R. Celajes.

I am a 4th-year college student pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science at New Era University, Quezon City, Philippines. With a passion for software and web development, I am continuously improving my skills in coding, design, and emerging technologies.

During my internship at Highly Succeed Inc., I served as an Assistant Team Leader in the Web Development Department, where I helped manage project workflows and contributed to website development. Additionally, I assisted the Graphics Design Department, creating visual assets for company campaigns and branding.

As an Intermediate Web Developer, I have experience working with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, TypeScript, and PHP, along with database management using MySQL and Firebase. I also have a background in mobile development using React Native and Java. Beyond development, I am expanding my knowledge in Machine Learning and AI applications.

With a blend of technical proficiency, leadership experience, and a keen eye for design, I am eager to continue learning and contributing to innovative projects.
